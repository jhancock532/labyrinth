Reflections

The pixels and the image we see are two different things.
Just as the reflection in a mirror is not the object itself,
Just as the map is not the territory.
What matters here is the story; the stories within stories, each hinting at other, larger stories.
Stories so large that they encompass life, the universe, you.
Gradually, we will see how everything is a story.

At least, everything that I've written here.
